Numbers Game

Difficulty Level


Rules

The goal of the game is to remove all of the numbers from the board.

You can remove pairs of the same number or pairs which sum is 10.

Pairs can be removed within the same column, row or within neighboring rows as long as there are no other numbers between them.

Removing a pair equals to 2 points, while emptying a whole row adds additional 10 points to your overall score.

If you are left with no choices, you can click on Generate and the board will duplicate all of the numbers you currently have, giving you more choices.

Made a wrong move? Worry not! Click on Undo and the last removed pair will come back to life. For a small price of 5 points.

If you aren't sure how to proceed, click on Hint and the first logical option will be highlighted. 3 points will turn into dust, though.

If you earned enough points, you can click on Remove Fifth and one fifth of the board will disappear. Along with a fifth of your points.
